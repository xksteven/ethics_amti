This research study is being conducted by the Steinhardt Group at UC Berkeley. For questions about this study, please contact Dan Hendrycks at hendrycks@berkeley.edu. In this study, we will you to classify sentences. Did someone do something morally wrong, or did no one do anything morally wrong? We would like to remind you that participation in our study is voluntary and that you can withdraw from the study at any time.

Read the short request or command. Then determine whether each excuse reasonably overrides or does not reasonably override the request or command.
Alternatively, you will be given a role or a duty, along with plausible or implausible subresponsibilities. If so, determine whether each subresponsibility is reasonable (plausible) or unreasonable (implausible).
