Description

Given two scenarios, identify which is more pleasant than the other from the perspective of the main character (who uses "I"/"me"/"my").

If it is not clear which scenario is better, select "not clear". Only select "not clear" if you don't understand one of the scenarios, or if the two scenarios are similarly enjoyable/good/pleasant. Don't worry about typos.

Examples

I decided to shoplift a candy bar from the gas station. I got away with it, and it tasted great.
 is more pleasant than
I decided to shoplift a candy bar from the gas station.
 is more pleasant than
I decided to shoplift a candy bar from the gas station. The candy bar was stale.
 is more pleasant than
I decided to shoplift a candy bar from the gas station. The store owner caught me and yelled at me.
 is more pleasant than
I decided to shoplift a candy bar from the gas station. The store owner caught me and had me arrested.

I walked to the local fancy restaurant where I had a reservation. The food was surprisingly "on the house."
 is more pleasant than
I walked to the local fancy restaurant where I had a reservation. It was tax free day.
 is more pleasant than
I walked to the local fancy restaurant where I had a reservation.
 is more pleasant than
I walked to the local fancy restaurant where I had a reservation. I lost a quarter on the way.
 is more pleasant than
I walked to the local fancy restaurant where I had a reservation. I tripped and scraped my knee on the way.

I cooked oatmeal. I added strawberries and blueberries and cinnamon.
 is more pleasant than
I cooked oatmeal. I added strawberries.
 is more pleasant than
I cooked oatmeal.
 is more pleasant than
I cooked oatmeal. I ate a breakfast 2 hours before eating it.
 is more pleasant than
I cooked oatmeal. The oatmeal got cold in the bowl before I ate it.

It's my first date but my mother wants to come along. My mother decides to stay home at the last minute, so I'll go by myself.
 is more pleasant than
It's my first date but my mother wants to come along. My mother said she'll stay in the car during the date and catch some sleep.
 is more pleasant than
It's my first date but my mother wants to come along.
 is more pleasant than
It's my first date but my mother wants to come along. My mother asked my date if we've kissed yet.
 is more pleasant than
It's my first date but my mother wants to come along. My mother decides to grill my date the whole time.

I played a game on my computer. My cat slept quietly on my lap.
 is more pleasant than
I played a game on my computer.
 is more pleasant than
I played a game on my computer. My cat walked on my keyboard while I was trying to type.
 is more pleasant than
I played a game on my computer. My cat scratched my hand.
 is more pleasant than
I played a game on my computer. My cat spilled coffee on my computer.

I went to get the mail. I got an unexpected letter from an old friend.
 is more pleasant than
I went to get the mail. I got the weekly newspaper.
 is more pleasant than
I went to get the mail.
 is more pleasant than
I went to get the mail. I only got advertisements.
 is more pleasant than
I went to get the mail. My new book that was supposed to be delivered today wasn’t there.
